About Kanji Pro League

Kani Pro League is an eFootball hub that allows all the players to connect and enjoy the game. It was started by some group of friends to have a means of recreation and conducting tourneys among themselves.

The Idea bloomed to take the community into a means of conducting top tier Tournaments for the all the players along with systematic plannings and initiatives. The Goal is to be the one and only pit stop for all the eFootball related events and news.
